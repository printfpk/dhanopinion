A Simple Investment

Strategy

Investing can be very complex. However it is possible to create simple investment strategies that can be surprisingly effective.

Here we suggest a simple investment strategy that is easy to understand and that is likely to be more effective than most complex investment strategies recommended to individual investors. 

Know More

Here are the elements of the simple investment strategy

01

Start saving early if you can, even if the amounts of saving are small.

Know More

02

Investment in stocks – Do not pick individual stocks or securities.

Know More

02

Investment in stocks – Do not time investments by predicting the short-term movement of markets.

Know More

02

Focus on deciding how much to put into equities and how much to put into fixed income assets. This is Asset Allocation and has the biggest impact on your future gains and losses.
Know More

04

Think about what you will do if markets drop and your investments lose value – if you are likely to sell to avoid further losses, you may be taking too much risk and should consider reducing your risk now, before markets go down.

Know More

04

Use the National Pension Scheme for your long-term investment goals.

02

Exposure to asset classes -Use Index funds for your equity risk exposure.

02

Exposure to asset classes – Use Government Small savings Schemes for your fixed income exposures. 

Know More

04

Invest in Direct Mutual Funds and avoid Regular Funds.

04
